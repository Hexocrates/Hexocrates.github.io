Biography

My Journey

Hi, I'm Stanley Adams, currently I am setting forth to break into the bug bounty for web applications. I have a bachelor’s degree in cyber security with a minor in digital forensics from the University of Texas at San Antonio and I am currently enrolled over at the SANS Technology Institute. I am currently interested in web security with a goal of progressing to finding flaws and securing them in APIs.

I am very active in the cyber security CTF community, grinding out challenges on the weekends, honing my skills and creating my own when I have some spare time. Practicing and working through these challenges allowed me to help my team as we worked together to score 8th place national in the National Cyber League Fall 2022 Team Game. Competing in these competitions I feel is one of the best ways to figure out what part of the cybersecurity world you fit in.
